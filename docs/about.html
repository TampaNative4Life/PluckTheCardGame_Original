About Pluck

Pluck is a three-player trick-taking card game built around pressure, quotas, and consequences.

Unlike games that rely on long scorekeeping, Pluck keeps the tension alive hand to hand. Miss your number and somebody earns the right to improve their next hand off yours.

That mechanic is what gives Pluck its edge. The table remembers. The next hand remembers. And the best players do more than count cards — they read people.

Why It Matters

Pluck is simple enough to learn quickly but sharp enough to keep strong players honest. It rewards awareness, table feel, and steady nerves.

Around the right table, it is not just a game. It becomes talk, memory, style, and reputation.
