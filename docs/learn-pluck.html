Learn Pluck in 60 Seconds

Three players. Seventeen tricks. No running score. Miss your number and somebody may get stronger off your hand next round.

1) The Deck

  • Start with a standard deck.
  • Remove 2♦, 2♥, and 2♠.
  • Keep 2♣.
  • Add both Jokers.
  • Total cards: 51.

2) The Job for Each Seat

  • Dealer needs 7 tricks.
  • Left of dealer needs 6.
  • Right of dealer needs 4.

3) How the Hand Starts

  1. First round has no plucks.
  2. After that, earned plucks happen before play begins.
  3. The dealer declares trump.
  4. The player with the 2♣ leads the first trick.

4) What a Pluck Is

If you finished above your target last hand, you may have earned plucks. A pluck means you pass a card face down to another player, and they must return their highest card in that same suit.

Example: You pass a low heart. They owe you their highest heart. That is not a misunderstanding. That is the game.

5) Trick Play

  • Follow suit if you can.
  • If you cannot, you may throw off or play trump.
  • You cannot lead trump until trump has been opened.
  • If clubs are trump, the 2♣ opens trump immediately.
  • The Big Joker and Little Joker are the highest trump cards.

6) The Whole Point

Make your number and stay safe. Fall short and next hand somebody may upgrade their hand using yours.

One-line summary: In Pluck, you are not just trying to win tricks. You are trying to avoid consequences.
