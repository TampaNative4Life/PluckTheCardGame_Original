Pluck Rules (Web Demo)

Game Flow

  1. Deal — 17 tricks worth of cards each (3 players).
  2. Pluck — happens only after a new deal. No auto-pluck. Plucks can be hairy.
  3. Trump Pick — happens AFTER plucks. Player with the most books to make (highest quota) calls trump based on their hand.
  4. Play — trick-taking. Winner leads next trick.

Key Rules (as implemented)

  • No mercy: falling behind means you get plucked.
  • Pluck failure is LOST (no re-pick).
  • No trump chosen before plucks: redeal → plucks → trump pick.
  • Joker rule: if you must play the joker, you play it (per your call).
  • Quota rotation: dealer rotates and quotas rotate with it.
