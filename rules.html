Pluck Rules (Quick Read)

Core Flow

Deal → Pluck → Trump Pick → Play

Dealer + Quotas (Rotating)

  • Dealer rotates RIGHT every deal (including first load).
  • Quotas are always: Dealer = 7, Left of Dealer = 6, Right of Dealer = 4.

Pluck Phase

  • Plucks happen after a new deal and before trump selection.
  • Pluck attempt can be LOST (no re-pick).
  • Plucker gives a low card in chosen suit; pluckee returns a higher card (same suit) if they have it.
  • No “auto pluck” — it can get hairy.

Trump

  • Trump is picked after plucks.
  • No “never no trump” (your rule: always a trump suit).
  • Jokers: must play joker (your rule callout).
