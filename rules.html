Pluck Rules (Web Demo)

Order of Play

Deal → Pluck → Trump Pick → Play

  • Dealer rotates RIGHT every deal (including first load).
  • Quotas rotate with dealer: Dealer=7, Left=6, Right=4.
  • No auto-pluck: Plucks can be hairy. You choose.
  • Trump is NOT chosen before plucks: Redeal → Plucks → Trump Pick.

Hard Lock (Rule Enforcement)

  • If you can follow suit, you must follow suit.
  • If trump is not open, you can’t lead trump when you still have non-trump.
  • On Trick 1, whoever has 2♣ leads Trick 1 with it.

This demo enforces “Hard Lock” by disabling illegal cards.

Pluck Notes

  • Wrong suit attempt = Pluck LOST (no re-pick).
  • Players who fall on hard times are just SOL—game has no mercy.
  • Player with 0 tricks is at the mercy of players above quota during pluck process.
  • After pluck exchange, hands must remain the correct size.
