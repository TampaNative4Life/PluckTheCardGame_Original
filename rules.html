Official Rules (Draft)

Players

Pluck is designed for 3 players.

Objective

Each player aims to meet their trick quota while avoiding unnecessary plucks.

Basic Round Flow

  1. Cards are dealt.
  2. Players declare or are assigned quotas.
  3. Tricks are played clockwise.
  4. Trick winners are determined.
  5. Pluck phase occurs based on quota results.
