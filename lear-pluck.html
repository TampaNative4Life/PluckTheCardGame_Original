Learn Pluck in 60 Seconds

Three players. Seventeen tricks. No running score. Miss your job and somebody gets paid back in cards.

1) The Setup

  • Use a standard deck.
  • Remove 2♦, 2♥, and 2♠.
  • Keep 2♣.
  • Add both Jokers.
  • That makes 51 cards total.

2) What Each Player Needs

  • Dealer needs 7 tricks.
  • Left of dealer needs 6 tricks.
  • Right of dealer needs 4 tricks.

3) What Happens First

  1. First round: no plucks.
  2. After that, earned plucks happen before the deal is played.
  3. Dealer then declares trump.
  4. The player with the 2♣ leads the first trick.

4) What Is a Pluck?

If you finished above your target last hand, you may have earned plucks. A pluck means you pass a card face down to another player, and they must give you back their highest card in that same suit.

Example: You pass a low heart. They owe you their highest heart. That is not bad luck. That is Pluck.

5) Trick Play

  • Follow suit if you can.
  • If you cannot, you may throw off or trump.
  • You cannot lead trump until trump has been opened.
  • If clubs are trump, the 2♣ opens trump immediately.
  • The Big Joker and Little Joker are the highest trump cards.

6) The Whole Point

Make your number and stay safe. Fall short and next hand somebody may reach into your future.

One-line version: In Pluck, you are not just trying to win tricks. You are trying to avoid consequences.
